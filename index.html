About

At Imperial we bake everything in house - pastries, breads, cake, cookies - if it's baked we bake it. Everything is baked fresh each day.

We work with local suppliers and source as many fresh ingredients we can directly from farmers. Our team of bakers is committed to making the best products with seasonal ingredients. Our selections of cakes, breads and pastries change frequently - please come check them out.

Our biggest variety of items is at Imperial Bakery but the cafes - Imperial on Fillmore, Imperial on Larkin and Toy Boat By Imperial - have great selections of pastries and breads each day that complement the Breakfast, Brunch and Lunch offerings.
